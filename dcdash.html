Greenlight Discharge Dashboard

Created webapp using Ruby on Rails based on contextual inquiry and rapid validation (pennlabs.uphs.upenn.edu:3005/). Partnered with senior quality leadership of Presbyterian Hospital and floor-based medicine team who had created checklist initiative with improvements in % patients discharge by noon, but had been steady without further improvement.

The initial request of a 'texting notification' solution was redirected towards a simple digital version of the working checklist, in order allow asynchronous communication of tasks required, between physician and discharge staff. Physician engagement incentives could be explored, by offering an alternative modality apart from direct phone calls. This solution was created as a prototype, as part of a staged integration into an existing, in-house workflow tool.


Version 1.0, deployed for use at Presbyterian Hospital. Launched in mid-July across 7 teams.
Dashboard enabled staff (SW, CRC, Pharmacy, PT) to asynchronously view checklist and delegate tasks.
